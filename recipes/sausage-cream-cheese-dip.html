Sausage Cream Cheese Dip

So simple, yet so tasty.

This is a dip that everyone seems to love. It's made in a Crock Pot using only three ingredients, so anyone can make it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Jimmy Dean breakfast sausage
  • 1 can of Rotel
  • 3 blocks of cream cheese

Steps

  1. Brown sausage meat in a skillet
  2. Add browned sausage to Crock Pot, along with the cream cheese and Rotel
  3. Set Crock Pot to high for approximately 1-2 hours
  4. Mix thoroughly
